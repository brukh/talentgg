Features

Build Teams from the Ground Up

We match you with other players that share your gaming preferences. Whether you're looking for a casual group to test off-kilter strategies, jockeying for position on the ranked ladder, or aiming for the pro scene, we're here to help!

Talent.gg was developed around the concept of bringing gamers together to achieve their aspirations. We leverage efficient algorithms that take the essence of you as a gamer – not just your skill level – and match you with other gamers. We've got the names, you just go kick some ass.
